Traditional governance approaches designed for older data ecosystems cannot scale to handle modern AI environments where data flows through prompts, agents, and continuously evolving workflows. Organizations must embed governance directly into operational infrastructure rather than treating it as a separate compliance function, leveraging new privacy automation agents to manage consent, assessments, and data subject requests at enterprise scale.
